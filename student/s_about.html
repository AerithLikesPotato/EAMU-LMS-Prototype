About EAMU Learning Hub

Empowering future leaders through quality education

East Asia Management University

Founded in 2019, East Asia Management University (EAMU) has been at the forefront of higher education in Cambodia. Our mission is to provide world-class education and foster innovation among the next generation of leaders.

The EAMU Learning Hub platform was created to extend our reach beyond the classroom, bringing quality education to students worldwide. With a focus on practical skills and industry-relevant knowledge, we prepare our students for successful careers in the global marketplace.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality education that empowers students to achieve their full potential and become leaders in their chosen fields.

Our Vision

To be Cambodia's leading educational institution, recognized globally for excellence in teaching, learning, and innovation.
